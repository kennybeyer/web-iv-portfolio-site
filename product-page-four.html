Nada Surf

Promotional Design

<

>

Nada Surf is one of my favorite bands and I decided to do a few promotional designs including a gig poster, a shirt, and a sticker for a made-up show at the Bourban in Lincoln, NE. The poster was inspired by the general feeling of solace I get from the band and influenced by one song in particular entitled 'Blizzard of '77'.
