Lucid

App Design

<

>

Dreams have always been really interesting to me and especially lucid dreaming. One method for lucid dreaming is to record your dreams after you wake up. I came up with this app concept called Lucid that lets you quickly and easily record your dreams by voice or text. You can then go back later and look at your dreams logged and it would tell you what your dreams might mean based on keywords.
